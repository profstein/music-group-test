Page Ready Checklist

Here are things to check to make sure your page is ready

  • BASE HTML: Do you have the base HTML elements? You can get these by typing ! and then tab and Emmet will write them for you.
  • EXTERNAL CSS: Do you have a link element that points to yo
  • THREE PAGE SECTIONS: Do you have three page sections: header, main, footer? Remember that header and footer should be repeated on each page.
    • Header: this should have your site title and navigation if you are using a traditional site navigation. You can set this up once and then copy/paste it across all of your pages.
    • Main: this element contains the page-specific content. Usually this is where your page title (h1 element) goes and then all of the page content for that page.
    • Footer: This is the information that is shared at the bottom of the page. I could contain things like copyright information. © Your Name
  • Page title: Do you have a page title in an h1 element?
  • Paragraph: do you have at least one paragraph of text in a p element?
  • Link: do you have at least one link in an a element? Your navigation counts for this.
  • Image: do you have at least one imaage. Did you attribute your image? See image example below.
